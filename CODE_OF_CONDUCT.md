# Code of Conduct

Welcome to the **Lumen Nightlies** community! This documentation repository thrives on collaboration and respect.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions.
- Assume good faith from other contributors.
- Focus on technical merit, not personal traits.
- Keep discussions on-topic for Lumen OS development.

## Expected Behavior

- Participate in an inclusive way for all skill levels.
- Share knowledge about ARM/mobile Linux builds.
- Help document Galaxy A05s testing and issues.
- Credit original authors when referencing work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks.
- Spam or off-topic posts unrelated to Lumen.
- Impersonation or deceptive practices.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings or removal from discussions. Contact maintainers for issues.

We aim to keep **Lumen Nightlies** a welcoming space for OS development!
